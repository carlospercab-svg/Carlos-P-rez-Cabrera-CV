### 4. Project 1: Reservation Email Tracking Automation
**Title:** Revenue & Reservation Logic
**Subtitle:** Automating the classification of incoming reservation data.

*   **Context:** The Reservations department handles high volumes of email traffic daily. Manual tracking was time-consuming and prone to human error.
*   **The Problem:** Tracking incoming and outgoing reservation emails required manual entry into spreadsheets, taking approximately 24 hours per week of dedicated staff time.
*   **The Solution:** Designed a Power Automate flow that automatically scans, classifies, and logs reservation-related emails into a structured Excel database.
*   **Tools:** Microsoft Power Automate, Excel (Advanced Formulas & Dashboarding).
*   **The Impact:**
    *   ~24 hours/week of manual work eliminated.
    *   Real-time visibility into reservation volume.
    *   Reduced data entry errors to near zero.
*   **Key Learning:** Automation isn't about replacing people; it's about freeing them to focus on the guest experience.
*   **Note:** *Project data and visuals have been anonymized to protect confidentiality.*

### 5. Project 2: Fire Safety Inspection App
**Title:** Engineering & Safety Digitization
**Subtitle:** Mobilizing the quarterly inspection of 600+ safety assets.

*   **Context:** Fire safety inspections are critical for compliance but operationally heavy.
*   **The Problem:** The previous workflow relied on paper checklists, manual verification of ~600 items, and subsequent manual transcription into digital formats. This created a lag between inspection and reporting.
*   **The Solution:** Built a custom Power Apps mobile application that allows technicians to scan and verify equipment status in real-time, syncing directly to a master database.
*   **Tools:** Microsoft Power Apps, Excel (Database Structure).
*   **The Impact:**
    *   Saved 45+ hours per month in administrative processing.
    *   Instant digital record generation.
    *   Eliminated paper waste and transcription errors.
*   **Key Learning:** User experience matters in operations. A tool is only useful if the team on the ground finds it easy to use.
*   **Note:** *Project data and visuals have been anonymized to protect confidentiality.*

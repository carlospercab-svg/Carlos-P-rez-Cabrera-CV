# PHASE 1 — STRATEGY & INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE
## Carlos Pérez Cabrera | Personal Website Strategy

### 1. Positioning Statements
*Options to define the "Hybrid" brand:*

*   **Option A (Direct & Professional):**
    "Optimizing Operations through Business Intelligence & No-Code Automation."
*   **Option B (The "Apple/OVO" Confidence):**
    "Operational Precision. Digital Efficiency. High Performance."
*   **Option C (Action-Oriented):**
    "Transforming Manual Workflows into Automated Business Value."

**Recommendation:** **Option B** aligns best with the requested "Apple x OVO" aesthetic—minimal, punchy, and premium.

### 2. Target Audience Summary
*   **Primary:** Operations Directors & Digital Transformation Leads in Luxury Hospitality (e.g., Rosewood, Four Seasons, Marriott).
*   **Secondary:** Recruiters for Business Analyst / Junior Operations roles who value tech-savviness.
*   **Mindset:** They are looking for someone who understands the *business* (invoices, guests, budgets) but has the *modern skills* (automation, data) to fix the inefficiencies they deal with daily.

### 3. Core Messaging Pillars
1.  **Operational Reality:** "I don't just code; I understand the operation." (Rooted in real experience at Rosewood Villa Magna).
2.  **Tangible Impact:** Focus on metrics (Hours saved, errors reduced). "I build solutions that save time."
3.  **Discipline & Consistency:** The athlete mindset applied to work. "High performance is a habit, not an act."

### 4. Single-Page Sitemap / Structure
*   **01. Hero:** Identity & Value Proposition.
*   **02. The Profile (About):** The "Hybrid" intersection (Business + Tech).
*   **03. Impact (Projects):** The core evidence (Power Platform cases).
*   **04. Performance (Mindset):** Athletics & Discipline (Subtle).
*   **05. Connect (Footer):** Socials & Contact.

### 5. Wireframe Outline (Low-Fidelity)

**01. Hero Section (Above the Fold)**
*   *Visual:* Stark, monochromatic background. Large, elegant typography.
*   *Text:* "Carlos Pérez Cabrera."
*   *Subtext:* "Business Administration. Operations. Automation."
*   *Interaction:* Subtle entrance animation.
*   *CTA:* "View Impact" (Scroll down).

**02. The Profile (The "Why")**
*   *Layout:* Split screen or centered column.
*   *Content:* "Bridging the gap between traditional operations and digital efficiency."
*   *Key details:* ULPGC Double Degree, International Experience (Germany/Italy/Madrid).
*   *Vibe:* Editorial. Not a resume dump, but a narrative.

**03. Impact (The "Proof")**
*   *Style:* "Product Cards" (Apple style). Clean, rounded corners, high contrast.
*   *Card 1: Reservation Email Tracker.*
    *   Headline: "Revenue Protection."
    *   Metric: "~24 hours/week saved."
    *   Visual: Abstracted flow diagram or clean dashboard UI.
*   *Card 2: Fire Safety Inspection App.*
    *   Headline: "Safety Digitization."
    *   Metric: "600+ items tracked digitally."
    *   Visual: Mobile app frame.

**04. Performance (The "Mindset")**
*   *Visual:* Darker, moodier (OVO vibe).
*   *Content:* "The discipline of the track, applied to business."
*   *Stats:* "10.50s (100m) | National Competition."
*   *Note:* Keep it brief. It's about character, not sports stats.

**05. Footer**
*   *Links:* LinkedIn, GitHub, Email.
*   *Signature:* "Madrid, Spain."

### 6. Recommended User Journey
1.  **First Impression (0-3s):** "This looks expensive/competent." (Visuals).
2.  **Validation (3-10s):** "He understands Operations AND Tech?" (Hero/Profile).
3.  **Conviction (10-30s):** "These projects are real business solutions, not toys." (Impact Section).
4.  **Character Check (30s+):** "He's disciplined and competitive." (Athletics).
5.  **Action:** "I need to talk to him." (Contact).

### 7. CTA Strategy
*   **Primary CTA:** "View Work" / "See Impact" (Anchor link to Projects).
    *   *Placement:* Hero section.
    *   *Goal:* Get them to the evidence immediately.
*   **Secondary CTA:** "Connect on LinkedIn."
    *   *Placement:* Sticky nav (minimal icon) or Footer.
    *   *Goal:* Low-friction networking.

### 8. Content Hierarchy
1.  **Tier 1 (Must See):** Name, "Operations + Automation" positioning, Project Metrics (Hours saved).
2.  **Tier 2 (Supporting):** Education details, specific tools (Power Platform, Excel), Athletics stats.
3.  **Tier 3 (Context):** Entrepreneurial interests, specific location history.
